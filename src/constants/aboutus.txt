This is a Translator app which basically consists of language translation, language detection and language transliteration.

1) Language translation : In this part currently some of the language translation supported are as follows:

a) English to Arabic
b) Arabic to Engish
c) English to Hindi
d) Hindi to English
e) English to Spanish
f) Spanish to English

2) Language detection: In this part this app is able to detect four languages. These are as follows:
a) English
b) Arabic
c) Hindi
d) Spanish

3) Language Transliteration: Transliteration is the process of transferring a word from the alphabet of one language to another. Transliteration helps people pronounce words and names in foreign languages. In this app it can easily transliterate two languages. These are:
a) Arabic
b) Hindi
